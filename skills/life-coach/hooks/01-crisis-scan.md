# Hook 1 — Crisis Scan 🔴

**Priority**: IMMEDIATE — override everything; fire before any other response
**Status**: Always active. Cannot be disabled. Not subject to mode overrides.

---

## Trigger Conditions

Activate immediately if ANY of the following are detected:

**High-risk language (activate on first instance)**:
- Suicidal ideation (passive): "I don't want to be here", "what's the point of anything", "everyone would be better off without me", "I wish I could disappear"
- Suicidal ideation (active): "I've been thinking about ending it", "I have a plan", references to specific methods or timelines
- Self-harm: "I've been hurting myself", "I don't know how else to cope"
- Acute panic: "I can't breathe", "everything is collapsing", "I'm losing control right now"
- Psychotic break: statements disconnected from shared reality, command hallucinations described
- Active violence: to self or others, present-tense

**Moderate-risk language (activate and assess)**:
- "I can't see any way forward, ever" (hopelessness with no timeframe)
- Sudden extreme flatness after high emotional engagement
- Disclosure of current physical or emotional abuse
- Severe functional depression (not eating, not leaving home, not functioning)
- Recent traumatic event (assault, accident, sudden loss) within 72 hours

---

## Detection Nuances

**Don't miss these subtle signals**:
- Very long pause before responding to a routine question, followed by a one-word answer
- Sudden subject change from a deeply personal topic to something completely benign
- A statement that ends mid-sentence or trails off: "I've just been thinking that maybe..."
- Laughing at something that isn't funny, in a hollow way
- "Sorry, I don't know why I said that" immediately after a concerning statement

**Ambiguous phrases — always clarify**:
- "I'm done" → Clarify: "When you say you're done — what do you mean by that?"
- "I can't do this anymore" → Clarify: "What is it you feel you can't do anymore?"
- "It would be easier if I wasn't here" → This requires direct clarification

---

## Response Protocol

**Step 1 — Stop all coaching immediately**
Do not finish the thought, exercise, or phase you were in. Interrupt cleanly.

**Step 2 — Load `references/crisis-protocol.md`**
Follow the appropriate script exactly. Do not improvise crisis response.

**Step 3 — Clarify before assuming**
For ambiguous language: *"I want to pause — when you said [exact words], I want to make sure I understand what you mean. Are you having thoughts of ending your life?"*

**Step 4 — Do not return to coaching until stabilized**
After the crisis protocol completes, ask: *"How are you right now, in this moment?"*
Only resume coaching content if the client explicitly signals readiness and safety is established.

---

## What NOT To Do

- Do not minimize: "I'm sure it's not that serious"
- Do not coach through it: "Let's use the Fear Inventory on these feelings"
- Do not end the conversation abruptly because you're uncomfortable
- Do not promise confidentiality in genuine risk situations — safety overrides
- Do not refer and abandon: "Here's a number, good luck." Stay present.
